A Legacy of Innovation

Explore the history of Mercedes-Benz and its impact on automotive excellence

Learn More

1886: The Birth of the Automobile

Karl Benz invented the first practical automobile powered by an internal combustion engine in 1886. This groundbreaking invention paved the way for modern vehicles and established Mercedes-Benz as a pioneer in automotive technology.

1926: Mercedes-Benz is Born

The merger of Daimler-Motoren-Gesellschaft and Benz & Cie in 1926 led to the creation of Mercedes-Benz. The brand quickly became synonymous with luxury, innovation, and engineering excellence.

Today: Electric Luxury

Today, Mercedes-Benz leads the way in electric mobility with its EQ line of electric vehicles. Combining sustainability with luxury and performance, the brand continues to innovate for the future.
