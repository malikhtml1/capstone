Did you Know that?

Mercedes-Benz stands out through a blend of innovation, luxury, and engineering precision. Its legacy includes the invention of the modern automobile.

MBUX (Mercedes-Benz User Experience) is an AI-powered infotainment system offering natural voice commands, touchscreen navigation, and intelligent personalization.

Yes, the Mercedes EQ lineup includes fully electric models designed to deliver performance and sustainability without compromising luxury.

The Mercedes name was inspired by Mercedes Jellinek, the daughter of Emil Jellinek, an early automobile dealer and influencer of the brand.

The first Mercedes-Benz car was built in 1926 after the merger of Daimler-Motoren-Gesellschaft and Benz & Cie, combining their automotive expertise.

The three-pointed star represents Mercedes-Benz's ambition to dominate land, sea, and air transportation with their engines and vehicles.
